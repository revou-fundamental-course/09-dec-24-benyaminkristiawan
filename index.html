Our Story

Established in 2020 and located in Kuningan, West Java, our bakery started from a passion for baking cakes and bread. What began as a simple hobby soon turned into a pre-order bakery business, fueled by the joy of creating homemade treats. Every process, from mixing to baking, is done with dedication—entirely by myself, with the loving support of my mother. Over time, our signature product, the bolen, has become a favorite among our customers, earning a special place in their hearts and kitchens.
